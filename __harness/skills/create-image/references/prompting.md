# Image prompting

## Garment study

Describe the object, view, material, construction, lighting, background, and what the image is for. Prefer an editorial product study over vague adjectives.

```text
Editorial studio product study of [object].
Preserve: [invariants].
Change: [one deliberate delta].
Material/construction: [specifics].
View and light: [specifics].
No logos, labels, text, watermarks, or recognizable signature graphics.
```

## Reference-conditioned edit

Make the current design the first reference. Explicitly separate invariants from the change and tell the model which secondary image informs which attribute.

## Editorial avatar

Use a legally reusable portrait reference. Request a clearly illustrated monochrome portrait with a neutral background, recognizable facial structure, no words or marks, and a specific texture such as restrained halftone or photocopier grain. Inspect the result for identity drift and label it as AI-generated.

Do not fabricate documentary events, uniforms, endorsements, or sensitive context around a real person.
