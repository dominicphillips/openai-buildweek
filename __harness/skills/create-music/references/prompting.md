# Music prompting

Order the brief as:

1. duration, genre, and era
2. mood, tempo, key, and meter
3. instrumentation and production
4. explicit `instrumental only` or a separate lyrics block
5. time-stamped or named sections
6. negative constraints that protect the intended use

For focus music, avoid attention-grabbing drops, long risers, lead solos, and abrupt transitions. Translate a living artist reference into audible properties such as drum texture, harmonic density, tempo, dynamics, stereo image, and arrangement.

Lyria 3 Pro accepts a prompt, up to ten optional images, and an optional seed. It produces one MP3 of variable length up to roughly three minutes; duration is guided through the prompt rather than guaranteed.
