# Undercover

Research snapshot: 2026-07-17. This is independent product grounding, not an endorsement, affiliation, or style-replication brief.

## Designer and documented point of view

**Jun Takahashi** founded and continues to design Undercover. The house's [official history](https://undercoverism.com/historyofu) traces the label from Tokyo and documents a practice that repeatedly places tailoring, street clothing, music, and narrative imagery in tension. Museum records consistently attribute the studied ensembles to Takahashi.

Neutral transferable traits:

- let a collection answer one narrative question through clothing rather than applied slogans alone;
- interrupt a tailored shape with one material, utility, or subcultural cue;
- use layered garments to reveal different readings in motion;
- keep graphic references attributable and separate from the garment's own construction logic.
