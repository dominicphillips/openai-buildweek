# Jil Sander

Research snapshot: 2026-07-17. This is independent product grounding, not an endorsement, affiliation, or style-replication brief.

## Designers and documented point of view

**Jil Sander** founded the house in Hamburg in 1968. **Simone Bellotti** has been creative director since March 2025, according to the [OTB brand profile](https://www.otb.net/en/jil-sander). That profile defines the house through design, quality, functionality, refined cuts, material innovation, and precise details.

Neutral transferable traits:

- remove elements only after identifying the function that must remain;
- make cut and material quality carry the visual interest;
- use contrast at a seam, edge, or gesture instead of filling every surface;
- test restraint against body movement and access, not only a still image.

## Product-use notes

Carry forward seam tolerances, closure effort, pocket access, material hand, and the minimum details needed for identity. Do not copy bag geometry, closures, wordmarks, a historic suit pattern, or the complete house palette and styling.
