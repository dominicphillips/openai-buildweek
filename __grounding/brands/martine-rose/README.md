# Martine Rose

Research snapshot: 2026-07-17. This is independent product grounding, not an endorsement, affiliation, or style-replication brief.

## Designer and documented point of view

**Martine Rose** is the London label's founder and designer. Her [official biography](https://www.martine-rose.com/pages/about) situates the practice in British menswear, while Nike's [football tailoring project](https://about.nike.com/en/newsroom/releases/nike-x-martine-rose-collection-redefines-women-s-football-styling) documents a gender-free wardrobe that shifts familiar football, office, and occasion-wear codes through proportion and context.

Neutral transferable traits:

- distort one familiar proportion while preserving recognizability;
- move details between work, sport, nightlife, and formal garments;
- design fit as a spectrum rather than a gendered binary;
- use styling tension to test whether an archetype can serve a new setting.
