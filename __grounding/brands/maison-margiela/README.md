# Maison Margiela

Research snapshot: 2026-07-17. This is independent product grounding, not an endorsement, affiliation, or style-replication brief.

## Designers and documented point of view

**Martin Margiela** founded the house in 1988 and established its formative practices of exposing construction, reusing existing garments, and questioning standardized bodies. **Glenn Martens** was appointed creative director in January 2025, according to the [official OTB announcement](https://assets.ctfassets.net/0p3ks596b6ik/5jHSPw3kFOmjJ2VqtBLxNm/1e4b956e1faa55631927c22b476a10d3/MAISON_MARGIELA_ANNOUNCEMENT.pdf).

Neutral transferable traits:

- reveal a seam, lining, allowance, or fitting device when it explains how the garment is made;
- repurpose an existing object by changing its use, not merely distressing its surface;
- treat wear and repair as evidence of time;
- interrogate the assumed body or scale embedded in a conventional pattern.
