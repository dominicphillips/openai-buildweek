# Fear of God

Research snapshot: 2026-07-17. This is independent product grounding, not an endorsement, affiliation, or style-replication brief.

## Designer and documented point of view

**Jerry Lorenzo** is founder and artistic director, as the official [Civil Collection Fall 2025 lookbook](https://fearofgod.com/en-bz/pages/the-civil-collection-fall-2025-lookbook) states. Its [Seventh Collection](https://showroom.fearofgod.com/pages/the-seventh-collection) joins American sportswear archetypes with tailoring, knitwear, and Italian fabrication, framing the wardrobe as a continuing system rather than a sequence of novelty items.

Neutral transferable traits:

- create ease for layering through dropped shoulders and controlled body volume;
- unify sportswear and tailoring through shared fabric, color, and proportion rules;
- reduce visible closures while keeping dressing intuitive;
- design a small set of compatible wardrobe shapes instead of isolated statements.
