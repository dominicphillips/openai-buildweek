# Our Legacy

Research snapshot: 2026-07-17. This is independent product grounding, not an endorsement, affiliation, or style-replication brief.

## Designers and documented point of view

Our Legacy was founded in Stockholm in 2005 by **Jockum Hallin, Cristopher Nying, and Richardos Klarén**, according to the [official company page](https://www.ourlegacy.com/info). The house describes custom-developed fabrics on recurring shapes, material repurposing, and a position between the familiar and irregular. Hallin is also identified as Work Shop creative director on the [official Timberland project](https://www.ourlegacy.com/work-shop-timberland).

Neutral transferable traits:

- keep a stable garment block while changing fabric, finish, and controlled ease;
- design patina as a documented process that continues with wear;
- use deadstock and archive fabric with provenance and realistic quantity limits;
- make one irregular detail legible against an otherwise familiar garment.

## Product-use notes

Carry forward fabric provenance, available meterage, shrinkage, wash recipe, fit measurements, and expected aging. Do not copy the Camion sole/zip arrangement, named blocks, Surfman graphics, exact treatments, or house styling.
