# Rhude

Research snapshot: 2026-07-17. This is independent product grounding, not an endorsement, affiliation, or style-replication brief.

## Designer and documented point of view

**Rhuigi Villaseñor** founded Rhude in Los Angeles in 2015 and remains identified as its creative director on the [official brand page](https://rh-ude.com/pages/information). That account describes ready-to-wear balancing luxury techniques with streetwear, using personal migration stories, American iconography, and Los Angeles culture as recurring material.

Neutral transferable traits:

- combine a relaxed sportswear base with one tailored finish or fabric upgrade;
- let personal geography inform a design without turning it into borrowed nostalgia;
- use wash and patina to establish time while preserving fit and durability;
- separate original narrative symbols from protected logos and found graphics.
