# Kiko Kostadinov

Research snapshot: 2026-07-17. This is independent product grounding, not an endorsement, affiliation, or style-replication brief.

## Designers and documented point of view

**Kiko Kostadinov** leads the label's menswear; the house's [AW21 menswear notes](https://kikokostadinov.com/collections/aw21-mens-text) describe an ongoing experiment in patternmaking that collides utility, formality, comfort, and artifice. **Laura and Deanna Fanning** design womenswear, as the official [SS21 womenswear notes](https://kikokostadinov.com/collections/ss21-womens-press-release) state, with a focus on shape and material development through contemporary and historical research.

Neutral transferable traits:

- articulate movement zones with curved seams and targeted volume;
- make utility garments modular without turning every detail into hardware;
- use color blocking to reveal pattern pieces and motion paths;
- combine one historic construction reference with a present-day use test.

## Product-use notes

Carry forward ergonomic questions: which seam follows a joint, which layer detaches, what range of motion improves, and what a color boundary explains. Do not copy a named garment, exact panel map, ASICS footwear geometry, runway palette, or complete look.
