# A-COLD-WALL*

Research snapshot: 2026-07-17. This is independent product grounding, not an endorsement, affiliation, or style-replication brief.

## Designer and documented point of view

**Samuel Ross** founded A-COLD-WALL* and established its formative industrial-design language; the [V&A](https://www.vam.ac.uk/articles/fashion-design-samuel-ross-a-cold-wall) describes the Converge Gilet as protective architecture joining sportswear, industrial design, and tailoring. The current [official brand statement](https://www.a-cold-wall.com/pages/brand) describes British industrial sportswear, experimental performance materials, and post-war industrial palettes but does not name a current individual creative director, so this dossier does not assign one.

Neutral transferable traits:

- expose load-bearing seams, panels, and fastening logic;
- choose materials by abrasion, weather, and movement requirements;
- convert architectural ideas into body-scale protection and access;
- use asymmetry only where it clarifies use or construction.
